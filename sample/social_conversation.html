Enabling Solution

Description

Enabling Solutions are 'systems of products, services and organisational tools that enable individuals or communities to achieve a result using at best their skills and abilities' (Manzini, 2004). ‘Instructables’ is an active DIY community where any individual can share their design projects and network with others regarding everyday life objects, abilities and skills.

Localisation

Description

One of the main issues of the unsustainable fashion and textile practices are linked to the scale of production and consumption and its use of resources. High volume production and consumption mean that we buy and discard more than ever (Fletcher, 2008). Dr Kate Fletcher deeply investigated various possibilities of the local design and wisdom. Her project captures the ‘local wisdom’, giving a platform to flourish and inspire.
